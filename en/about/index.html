About

Presentation of Eclipium

Eclipium is a video game creation studio founded in 2019 by Raouf O., it offers puzzle, tactical and logic games. You will find free games, fun and to pass the time. Our team works hard to provide you with qualitative games and quality graphics, pleasant for your eyes. We take care of our game-design and level-design, to always present you an original gameplay as well as worked and fantastic levels. Of course we are extremely active on Discord, thanks to our new ticket system with a Discord bot, you just have to send a message and a staff member will be there to help you as soon as possible. We’re on the lookout, we look at all suggestions and respond to them. And we are also looking for staff, if you want to join us, don’t hesitate to apply.
